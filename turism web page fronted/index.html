Welcome to Balochistan the land of Mountains

Explore the hidden beauty of Pakistan's largest province.

Must-Visit Places

Chiltan - Famous for the mysterious Chiltan range and its legendary folklore.

Kalat - A historical city known for its cultural heritage and the royal palace.

Ziarat - Home to Pakistan's oldest juniper forest and the historic Quaid-e-Azam Residency.

Charo Machi - A hidden gem with lush green valleys and scenic beauty in Balochistan.

Hingol National Park - A breathtaking landscape with unique rock formations.

Kund Malir Beach - One of the most scenic beaches in Pakistan.

Mehrgarh - One of the earliest sites of human civilization.

Mula Chotok - A hidden oasis with stunning waterfalls and breathtaking scenery.

Nomadic Lifestyle

Many tribes in Balochistan live a semi-nomadic life, herding livestock and practicing ancient traditions.

Balochi Cuisine

Balochi Sajji, a roasted lamb dish, is a famous delicacy enjoyed across Pakistan.
